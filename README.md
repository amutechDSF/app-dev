# *Cars* (2006)

> *"Life is a journey. Enjoy the trip."*  
> — *Cars* tagline

## **Overview**

*Cars* is a 2006 American computer-animated sports comedy film produced by **Pixar Animation Studios** and released by **Walt Disney Pictures**.

- **Directed by**: John Lasseter  
- **Produced by**: Darla K. Anderson  
- **Starring**: Owen Wilson, Paul Newman, Bonnie Hunt, Larry the Cable Guy  
- **Genre**: Animation, Comedy, Family  
- **Runtime**: 117 minutes

---

## **Plot Summary**

Lightning McQueen, a hotshot rookie race car, finds himself stranded in **Radiator Springs**, a small town on Route 66, on his way to the big Piston Cup Championship in California. There, he meets colorful characters who help him realize there's more to life than fame and trophies.

---

## **Main Characters**

- **Lightning McQueen** – A fast, ambitious race car.  
- **Mater** – A rusty but lovable tow truck.  
- **Doc Hudson** – A retired race car with a mysterious past.  
- **Sally Carrera** – A Porsche and McQueen's love interest.

---

## **Why I Like It**

- **Stunning Animation**: Pixar's animation makes each car expressive and environments immersive.  
- **Great Message**: It teaches the importance of friendship, humility, and enjoying the journey.  
- **Memorable Characters**: Mater and Doc are especially well-developed and endearing.

---

## **Fun Fact**

- *Cars* was **Paul Newman's** last feature film role before his death in 2008.
